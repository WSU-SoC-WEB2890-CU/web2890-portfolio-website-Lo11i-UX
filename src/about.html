About Me

Hi! I'm Lauren

Passionate UX designer and marketer. I create and blend data analytics into my work so each decision supports users andbusiness goals. I'm passionate about helping all users have the best possible experience.

My Story

I chose Web and User Experience because I love the mix of creativity and problem solving. I started in traditional web courses, but quickly realized I was most excited about how people actually feel when they use a product. That led me to focus on UX, accessibility, and making digital experiences easier for everyone to use.

Right now, I'm a WEB/UX student at Weber State University, building projects that blend research, accessibility standards, and visual design. My background in marketing, detail-oriented work, and accessibility gives me a slightly different angle than a typical "just design" student - I care about both the story and the data behind every decision.

I'm most passionate about helping real people feel less frustrated with technology. Whether that's simplifying a form, improving color contrast, or reworking a layout, I want users to feel calm, confident, and supported when they interact with my work.

My Process

1. Plan & Goals

I collaborate with stakeholders to clarify goals, constraints, and measures of success so we know what "good" looks like.

2. Research

I talk to users, review data, and audit existing flows to understand pain points and opportunities.

3. Design

I move from sketches to wireframes to hi-fi prototypes.

4. Launch

I partner with devs to hand off specs, ensure accessibility and gather feedback for future iterations.
